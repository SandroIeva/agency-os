# Brand Intelligence

### Supported providers

i7OS supports:

* Claude by Anthropic
* ChatGPT by OpenAI
* Gemini by Google

Available models can depend on the provider account and configuration connected to your workspace.

### Connect a provider

Open **Settings → AI models** and select a provider. Follow the instructions shown for the selected connection method.

Only workspace members with the required permissions should manage provider credentials.

### How context works

Brand Intelligence receives context based on the area you are currently using. For example, it can understand whether you are working in a brand, project, brainstorm, document, or planning view.

Add complete brand foundations before asking for highly specific on-brand output. Better context produces more useful drafts and recommendations.

### Useful tasks

You can use Brand Intelligence to:

* explore a brand strategy
* summarize research
* draft a brief
* generate or refine content
* analyze a channel or audience
* create ideas for a campaign
* turn a discussion into next steps
* generate images when the selected provider supports it

### Writing a useful request

A strong request usually includes:

1. the desired outcome
2. the intended audience
3. the relevant channel or format
4. important brand constraints
5. the definition of a good result

Example:

> Draft three launch-post concepts for our new service. Use the current brand voice, speak to independent creative teams, and keep each concept suitable for LinkedIn.

### Review before publishing

AI output should be treated as a draft. Review facts, claims, tone, and visual details before sharing or publishing the result.
