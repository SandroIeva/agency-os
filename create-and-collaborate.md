# Create and collaborate

### Brainstorm boards

Brainstorm is an infinite collaborative canvas for visual thinking.

Boards can include:

* sticky notes and text
* shapes, arrows, and freehand drawing
* images, stickers, and emoji
* comments and mentions
* connected mind-map branches

Team members can work on the same board in real time. Use comments and mentions when an idea needs feedback or an owner.

### Moodboards

Create moodboards to collect visual references, images, colors, and notes.

You can:

* upload images
* add image or website URLs
* organize references on a grid or canvas
* add tags and notes
* extract and review color palettes

### Documents

Use documents for briefs, concepts, guidelines, research, and structured brand knowledge.

Documents support collaboration through comments, mentions, folders, activity, and sharing. Keep a document inside the relevant brand or project so the surrounding context remains clear.

### Assets and creations

Uploaded and AI-generated images can be organized as brand assets. Filter content by format, use folders where helpful, and send selected outputs to Google Drive.

### Social media posts

Create social content from connected brand context. Choose channels, prepare a visual, write the caption, and publish immediately or schedule the post.

For the complete workflow, see Social media.
