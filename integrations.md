# Integrations

### Google Drive

Connect Google Drive to:

* select individual files
* connect a shared folder
* import images, videos, and documents
* upload i7OS assets to Drive
* keep files linked to the work they support

If Drive access expires, reconnect your Google account in **Settings**.

### Google Calendar

Google Calendar integration allows i7OS to display and create calendar events. It is also used for reminders that should appear across your devices.

### AI providers

Connect Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini in **Settings → AI models**. The selected provider is then available to the i7OS assistant and supported creative workflows.

See Brand Intelligence for guidance.

### Social channels

Connect supported social accounts to review performance and publish content.

Supported connections currently include:

* Instagram
* LinkedIn
* Threads
* X
* Pinterest

The exact analytics available can vary by platform and account type.

### Access and permissions

Some integrations can only be connected or disconnected by a workspace administrator. This protects shared accounts and prevents individual project members from changing organization-wide connections.

### Troubleshooting

If an integration stops working:

1. confirm that you are signed into the correct external account
2. reconnect the service in Settings
3. check that your workspace role permits the action
4. retry the action after the connection is refreshed
